MERCURY
Mercury is the smallest planet in the Solar System and the closest to the Sun. orbit around the Sun takes 87.97 Earth days, the shortest of all the Sun's planets. Despite its proximity to the Sun, Mercury is not the hottest planet in our solar system that title belongs to nearby Venus.
